Sewai

Prep Time: 20 Minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Roasted Vermicelli
  • Full-fat Milk
  • Sugar and Cardamom powder

Instructions

  1. Boil milk and reduce it slightly.
  2. Add vermicelli and cook until soft.
  3. Stir in sugar and garnish with chopped nuts.
