Paneer Tikka

Prep Time: 30 Minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 250g Paneer cubes
  • Yogurt and Ginger-garlic paste
  • Bell peppers and Onions

Instructions

  1. Marinate paneer and veggies in spiced yogurt for 2 hours.
  2. Skewer them alternatingly.
  3. Grill in an oven or tawa until charred.
