Tandoori Roti

Prep Time: 15 Minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat flour (Atta)
  • Water and Salt
  • Butter for glazing

Instructions

  1. Knead a soft dough and let it rest.
  2. Roll into circles and apply water on one side.
  3. Stick to a hot tawa and flip to cook over a direct flame.
