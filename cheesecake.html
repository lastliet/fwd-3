Cheesecake

Prep Time: 60 Minutes

Difficulty: Hard

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese
  • Graham Cracker crumbs
  • Sugar and Vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Press crumbs into a pan to form the crust.
  2. Beat cream cheese with sugar and pour over crust.
  3. Bake in a water bath and chill for 4 hours.
