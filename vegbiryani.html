Veg Biryani

Prep Time: 45 Minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • Mixed vegetables (Carrots, Peas, Beans)
  • Biryani Masala and Whole Spices

Instructions

  1. Soak rice for 30 minutes and parboil with spices.
  2. Sauté vegetables with yogurt and biryani masala.
  3. Layer rice over vegetables and dum-cook for 15 minutes.
